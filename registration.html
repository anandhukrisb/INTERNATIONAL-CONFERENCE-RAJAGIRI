REGISTRATION

Welcome to the registration page for the 10th International Conference for Social Work in Health and Mental Health. All persons intending to attend the Conference must complete an online Registration Form.

Please read the below registration information before you complete the Registration Form. Should you experience any difficulties please contact the Conference Managers at aasw@forumgroupevents.com.au.

REGISTRATION INCLUSIONS

In-Person Registration

  • Access to all Keynote and Concurrent Sessions
  • Access to Workshops (included on the main program)
  • Access to Exhibition Hall
  • Lunch & Morning & Afternoon Tea
  • One ticket to the Welcome Reception
  • Access to on-demand session recordings (3 months)
  • Certificate of Attendance & CPD Hours

Online Registration

  • Live stream of sessions held in the Plenary Room
  • Access to on-demand recordings for 3 months
  • Access to virtual exhibition components
  • Certificate of Attendance
  • CPD Hours available
  • Interactive Q&A for live sessions

MORE INFORMATION:

Registration Confirmation −

Your completed registration and successful payment will be acknowledged via email. Your registration will only be processed if payment has been received.

Payment Options −

Please note all online registrations require immediate payment by credit card. Accepted credit cards are MasterCard, Visa and American Express. Please note all transactions by credit card will appear on your statement as payment to ‘Forum Group Events'.

Cancellation/Postponement −

All online bookings are non-cancellable, and any online Registration Fees paid are non-refundable.

In-person registration cancellations will not be accepted unless made in writing to the Conference Organisers at aasw@forumgroupevents.com.au.

Conditions Charges Applicable
On or before 30 June 2024 100% refund less 10% administration fee
On or before 4 November 2024 50% refund less 10% administration fee
After 4 November 2024 No refund available
No refund will be paid following failure to attend without notice

In the unlikely event that we have to cancel the event, if, for example, delegate numbers are too low to run the event, we will give you as much notice as possible, and you will be given the option of a refund or transfer to an alternative event.

We are not liable to you for any non-attendance for any reason. If you are unable to attend the event, event presentations will be available for on-demand viewing after the Conference. Please consult the Event website or contact us for further details.
