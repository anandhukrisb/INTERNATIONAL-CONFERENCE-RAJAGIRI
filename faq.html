Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the ICSWHMH 2027 Conference. If you can't find what you're looking for, please feel free to contact us.

How do I register for the conference? +

Visit the Registration page on our website and complete the online form. Early bird rates are available until [date]. For group registrations, please contact us directly for special pricing.

Can I attend virtually? +

Yes! The 2027 ICSWHMH is a hybrid event. Virtual attendance options include live-streamed keynotes, access to select sessions, and networking opportunities. Virtual registration is available at a reduced rate.

When is the abstract submission deadline? +

Abstract submissions close on [date]. All submissions will be reviewed by our scientific committee, and you'll receive notification of acceptance by [date]. You can submit your abstract on the Abstract Submission page.

Are accommodation recommendations available? +

Yes, we have partnered with several hotels near the venue offering special conference rates. Visit our Accommodation page for details and booking codes.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance? +

All registered attendees will receive a digital certificate of attendance after the conference. CPD points may be available for eligible professionals.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellations made 60+ days before receive a full refund minus admin fee. 30-59 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: registration can be transferred to another person.
